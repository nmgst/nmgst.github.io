Neil Moran

Welcome to Neil Moran Golf

My name is Neil Moran. I was born in Bromborough on the Wirral peninsula. I was a member of Bromborough Golf Club for eight years, in this time I was the junior and colts club captain. I was advised by Bromborough club professional Geoff Berry to join the PGA training programme. I became a fully qualified AAA PGA professional in 1998. I was invited to the Netherlands by the Twentsche Golfclub in 1999 to become a full time coach/playing professional.

Seventeen years experience:
Bromborough Golf Club (teaching/playing professional, Great Britain);
Head teaching professional Dural golf driving range, Sydney Australia;
Teaching professional at the Twentsche Golfclub, Netherlands;
Teaching professional at de Koepel Wierden, Netherlands for two years;
Head professional golf at Golf Duinzicht The Hague, Netherlands, present day.
