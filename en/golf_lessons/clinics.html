Clinics

For companies and sponsors I am organizing a clinic program that lasts for three hours.

The program includes:
Opening speech;
Warm up;
Demonstration lesson;
Practice the swing;
Competition for chipping;
Competition for putting;
Competition for the "longest drive";
Meal at the end of the event.

Prices depend on group size. Large groups will be split into smaller groups, each will be trained by a professional.

Note Maximum 12 persons for one professional.

Example three hour clinic

Program description
Start time: 15.00;
14.45 coffee/drink;
Assemble at the driving range 15.00;
Opening speech and group warming up;
Followed by an explanation and demonstration;
15.30-16.10 driving range experience;
16.15-16.50 chipping experience;
16.50-17.00 toilet break or a quick drink;
17.00-17.30 putting demonstration/10 hole putting competition;
17.00-18.00 driving range- longest drive experience/competition/prize giving and closing speech.
